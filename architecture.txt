front-end => validator => translator => back-end

front-end:

- handles ALL SMTP I/O, including CR/NL conversion

- parses commands, and dispatches them to the back-end

validator:

- makes sure that domain names, senders, and recipients are valid

translator:

- translates sender or recipient names on the fly

back-end:

- invokes the mail storage / queueing agent (ex qmail-queue)
